Welcome to my hub!

I'm an incoming CSE PhD student at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, focusing on developing efficient infrastructure to support agentic workloads. I'm broadly interested in distributed systems, database systems, and modern hardware. I'm fortunate enough to be supervised by Prof. Eric Lo.

Previously, I was a computer science undergraduate student at the University of Toronto, where I was introduced to the field of hardware-accelerated database by Prof. Qizhen Zhang. I also spent around a year at Prof. Jialin Li's lab where I worked with Dr. Chaoyi Ruan, without whom I won't realise the fascinating power of LLM agents and recognise the vast unexplored space of AI infrastructure.
